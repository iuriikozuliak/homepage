I’m 23 year old ukrainian guy living and working in great city of Wroclaw.

It all started back in 2006 when I created my first web page for my own local ISP. Since that time I have worked hard for a huge variety of clients, from small design studios to international companies all over the world.

After graduating with Bachelor Degree in 2010 worked as a freelance web developer for a whole year. Starting to work, especially in low-budget segment, on russian freelance market could be a true nightmare, but I got excellent communication and problem solving skills. I worked on different projects from Facebook applications to large web sites for companies. For a few months even was in top 3 best developers on #2 largest russian online workplace.

Also I got some experience of backend programming . Maybe I’m not an expert on it, but can work with full technology stack, and can either move closer toward the back-end or to the user interface.

In july 2012 I graduated at the Ternopil National University with Master’s Degree, moved to Poland and started an Front End Developer at Clearcode. And now I can speak on 4 different languages, well, maybe my polish is not perfect, but I’m working on it.

I’m enjoing the process of turning great design into precise layouts, write the code necessary for pixel-perfect UI/UX and bridging the gap between designers and developers.
